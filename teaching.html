Teaching and Mentoring

My goal is to help students and colleagues build strong quantitative skills through active, project-based learning. My teaching and mentoring activities focus on data analysis and visualization, reproducible research methods, and literate programming, with a secondary emphasis on probability and statistics. Specific projects include simulation modeling of dynamical systems, time series analysis, and disease ecology.
